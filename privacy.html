1. 信息收集

GameZone 是一个纯前端的在线游戏平台。我们不收集任何个人身份信息，不要求注册或登录。

为了提升您的使用体验，我们使用浏览器的本地存储（localStorage）保存以下信息：

  • 您设置的昵称和头像（随机生成，可自定义）
  • 主题颜色和语言偏好
  • 游戏收藏列表和最近玩过的游戏
  • 游戏排行榜分数和评论内容
  • 游戏录像数据（存储在浏览器 IndexedDB 中）

以上数据完全保存在您的浏览器中，我们无法访问也不会传输到任何服务器。清除浏览器缓存将删除所有数据。

2. Cookie 使用

本站不使用 Cookie。所有偏好设置通过 localStorage 存储在您的设备上。

1. Information Collection

GameZone is a purely front-end online gaming platform. We do not collect any personally identifiable information and do not require registration or login.

To enhance your experience, we use your browser's local storage (localStorage) to save the following:

  • Your nickname and avatar (randomly generated, customizable)
  • Theme color and language preferences
  • Game favorites and recently played games
  • Leaderboard scores and comments
  • Game recordings (stored in browser IndexedDB)

All data is stored entirely in your browser. We cannot access it, and it is never transmitted to any server. Clearing your browser cache will delete all data.

2. Cookies

This site does not use cookies. All preferences are stored via localStorage on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
